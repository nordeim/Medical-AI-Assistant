## üöÄ Start Your Consultation in 5 Minutes

### Step 1: Access the System (1 minute)
1. Open your web browser
2. Go to your healthcare provider's Medical AI Assistant link
3. Click "Start New Consultation"

### Step 2: Provide Consent (1 minute)
1. Read the privacy notice (it's short and important)
2. Check the consent boxes:
   - ‚úì I consent to AI-assisted consultation
   - ‚úì I understand my privacy rights
3. Click "Begin Consultation"

### Step 3: Start Your Conversation (2 minutes)
The AI will greet you and ask: *"Hello! Can you tell me what brings you here today?"*

**Respond with your main concern:**
- "I have a headache"
- "I'm feeling chest discomfort"
- "I've been feeling dizzy"

### Step 4: Answer Questions (1 minute)
The AI will ask follow-up questions. **Be honest and specific:**

**Good responses:**
- "The headache started yesterday"
- "It's a dull ache on the right side"
- "It's a 6 out of 10 in pain level"

### Emergency? STOP and Call 911
If you have these symptoms, call 911 immediately:
- Severe chest pain
- Trouble breathing
- Stroke signs (facial drooping, arm weakness, speech difficulty)
- Severe bleeding

## ‚ùì Quick Answers to Common Questions

**Q: Is this safe?**
A: Yes! Your information is encrypted and only seen by your healthcare team.

**Q: Will I get a diagnosis?**
A: No. The AI gathers information for healthcare professionals to review.

**Q: What if I don't understand a question?**
A: Ask the AI to explain or rephrase the question.

**Q: Can I stop anytime?**
A: Yes, you can end the consultation at any time.

**Remember:** Always seek immediate help for serious symptoms, even if the AI doesn't flag them.
