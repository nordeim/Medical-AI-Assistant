## PR Campaign Strategy

### Launch Campaign Framework

#### New Product/Service Launch
**Pre-Launch (60 days before):**
- Teaser campaign with key influencers
- Analyst briefing sessions
- Media embargo coordination
- Customer reference identification

**Launch Week:**
- Press release distribution
- Media interviews and briefings
- Social media campaign activation
- Analyst call and presentation

**Post-Launch (30 days after):**
- Follow-up media outreach
- Case study development
- Success story amplification
- Analyst report follow-up

#### Executive Leadership Announcement
**Announcement Timing:**
- Quarterly earnings periods
- Industry conference cycles
- Major partnership announcements
- Award recognition moments

**Content Development:**
- Executive bio and backgrounders
- Leadership philosophy statements
- Vision and strategy documents
- Industry position papers

### Crisis Communication Strategy

#### Crisis Response Framework
**Assessment Level 1 - Minor Issues:**
- Social media monitoring response
- Direct customer communication
- Internal team notification
- Documentation and tracking

**Assessment Level 2 - Moderate Issues:**
- Executive team mobilization
- Legal counsel engagement
- Media response preparation
- Stakeholder communication plan

**Assessment Level 3 - Major Issues:**
- Full crisis team activation
- External PR counsel engagement
- 24/7 response team establishment
- Regulatory notification requirements

#### Crisis Communication Protocols
**Response Timeline:**
- Immediate acknowledgment (within 2 hours)
- Initial response statement (within 6 hours)
- Detailed response plan (within 24 hours)
- Follow-up and updates (ongoing)

**Message Development:**
- Fact-based, transparent communication
- Customer-first priority messaging
- Regulatory compliance emphasis
- Corrective action commitment
