## Quality Assurance Framework

### Content Review Process
1. Technical accuracy review by subject matter experts
2. Legal and compliance review
3. Brand voice and messaging consistency check
4. Final approval by executive leadership

### Fact-Checking Standards
- All medical claims verified with peer-reviewed sources
- Statistical data cross-referenced with original studies
- Regulatory information verified with official sources
- Expert quotes confirmed with original speakers

### Ethical Guidelines
- Transparent disclosure of conflicts of interest
- Balanced presentation of research findings
- Respectful treatment of competing viewpoints
- Commitment to accuracy over sensationalism
