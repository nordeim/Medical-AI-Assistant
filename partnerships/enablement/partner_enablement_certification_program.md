# Healthcare Partnership Enablement and Certification Program

## Program Overview

The Partnership Enablement and Certification Program provides comprehensive training, certification, and ongoing development for all healthcare partners. This program ensures partners have the knowledge, skills, and resources needed to effectively sell, implement, and support healthcare technology solutions while maintaining high standards of customer satisfaction and compliance.

## Partner Enablement Strategy

### Enablement Framework

#### Three-Tier Enablement Approach
**Tier 1: Foundation Certification**
- **Target Audience**: All partner sales and technical professionals
- **Duration**: 40 hours (5 days) comprehensive training
- **Investment**: Included in partnership agreement
- **Outcome**: Basic certification for sales and technical competencies

**Tier 2: Advanced Specialization**
- **Target Audience**: Partner subject matter experts and consultants
- **Duration**: 80 hours (10 days) advanced training
- **Investment**: $2,500 per participant
- **Outcome**: Advanced certification in specific solution areas

**Tier 3: Expert Mastery**
- **Target Audience**: Partner solution architects and technical leads
- **Duration**: 120 hours (15 days) expert-level training
- **Investment**: $5,000 per participant
- **Outcome**: Expert certification and master-level competency

#### Blended Learning Approach
**Online Learning (40%)**:
- **Self-Paced Modules**: Interactive online learning modules
- **Virtual Classrooms**: Live virtual classroom sessions
- **On-Demand Resources**: 24/7 access to learning resources
- **Mobile Learning**: Mobile-optimized learning experiences

**Instructor-Led Training (35%)**:
- **Live Workshops**: Interactive workshops and seminars
- **Hands-On Labs**: Practical hands-on laboratory sessions
- **Peer Learning**: Collaborative learning and knowledge sharing
- **Expert Sessions**: Sessions with industry experts and thought leaders

**Practical Application (25%)**:
- **Real-World Projects**: Real-world project implementations
- **Mentorship Programs**: Mentorship with experienced professionals
- **Case Studies**: Analysis of real customer scenarios
- **Simulation Exercises**: Healthcare scenario simulations

## Certification Programs

### Sales Certification Program

#### Certified Healthcare Technology Sales Professional (CHTSP)
**Certification Level**: Foundation
**Validity**: 2 years
**Continuing Education**: 20 hours annually

**Certification Requirements**:
- Complete 40-hour sales training program
- Pass comprehensive sales certification exam (85% minimum score)
- Demonstrate proficiency in sales scenario simulations
- Complete 3 shadowed sales calls with feedback
- Achieve customer satisfaction score of 90%+ on joint calls

**Exam Components**:
- **Healthcare Industry Knowledge** (25%): Industry dynamics, market trends, regulatory environment
- **Product Knowledge** (30%): Solution portfolio, capabilities, competitive positioning
- **Sales Methodology** (25%): Sales process, techniques, best practices
- **Customer Scenarios** (20%): Real-world customer scenarios and solutions

**Recertification Requirements**:
- Complete 20 hours of continuing education annually
- Pass recertification exam (80% minimum score)
- Demonstrate ongoing customer success
- Participate in quarterly knowledge updates

#### Advanced Healthcare Sales Expert (AHSE)
**Certification Level**: Advanced
**Validity**: 3 years
**Prerequisites**: CHTSP certification + 1 year field experience

**Certification Requirements**:
- Complete 80-hour advanced sales training program
- Pass advanced sales certification exam (90% minimum score)
- Lead 5 customer presentations with 85%+ satisfaction
- Develop 3 comprehensive healthcare ROI analyses
- Mentor 2 junior sales professionals

**Exam Components**:
- **Advanced Sales Strategy** (30%): Strategic account planning, enterprise sales
- **Healthcare Economics** (25%): Healthcare economics, reimbursement, value-based care
- **Complex Sales Scenarios** (25%): Complex sales situations and resolution strategies
- **Leadership and Mentoring** (20%): Leadership skills and team development

#### Healthcare Sales Master (HSM)
**Certification Level**: Expert
**Validity**: 3 years
**Prerequisites**: AHSE certification + 2 years field experience

**Certification Requirements**:
- Complete 120-hour master-level training program
- Pass master certification exam (95% minimum score)
- Achieve $5M+ in annual sales for 2 consecutive years
- Lead successful implementation of complex sales methodology
- Speak at industry events or contribute to thought leadership

**Exam Components**:
- **Strategic Sales Leadership** (35%): Strategic thinking, market leadership
- **Innovation and Vision** (30%): Innovation, future trends, strategic vision
- **Thought Leadership** (20%): Industry thought leadership and expertise
- **Mentorship and Development** (15%): Developing others and building teams
