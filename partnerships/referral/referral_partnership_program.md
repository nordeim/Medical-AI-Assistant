# Healthcare Referral Partnership Program

## Program Overview

The Referral Partnership Program targets medical consultants, healthcare advisors, industry experts, and thought leaders who can provide qualified introductions and referrals to healthcare decision-makers. This program focuses on relationship-based selling and trusted advisor referrals.

## Target Referral Partners

### Medical Consultants & Advisors
**Target Partners**: Former healthcare executives, clinical advisors, healthcare strategy consultants
- **Profile**: 20+ years healthcare experience, extensive network
- **Compensation**: 15-25% referral fees
- **Revenue Potential**: $1M-$5M annually per partner

#### Partner Types:
- **Former Hospital CEOs/CMOs**: Senior healthcare executives with extensive networks
- **Clinical Advisors**: Practicing physicians with consulting practices
- **Healthcare Consultants**: Strategy and operations consultants
- **Industry Analysts**: Healthcare IT analysts and thought leaders

### Healthcare Advisors & Brokers
**Target Partners**: Healthcare investment bankers, M&A advisors, healthcare brokers
- **Profile**: Transaction-focused advisors with healthcare transaction experience
- **Compensation**: 10-20% referral fees
- **Revenue Potential**: $500K-$3M annually per partner

#### Partner Types:
- **Investment Bankers**: Healthcare-focused investment banking professionals
- **M&A Advisors**: Healthcare mergers and acquisition advisors
- **Healthcare Brokers**: Healthcare facility and technology brokers
- **Financial Advisors**: Healthcare-focused financial advisors

### Technology Vendors & Resellers
**Target Partners**: Complementary technology vendors, hardware resellers, service providers
- **Profile**: Adjacent technology providers with established relationships
- **Compensation**: 12-18% referral fees
- **Revenue Potential**: $300K-$2M annually per partner

#### Partner Types:
- **Complementary Vendors**: Non-competing technology vendors
- **Hardware Resellers**: Healthcare hardware and infrastructure providers
- **Service Providers**: IT services, consulting, and support providers
- **Integration Partners**: System integrators and connectivity providers

### Professional Services Firms
**Target Partners**: Law firms, accounting firms, healthcare consulting firms
- **Profile**: Professional service providers with healthcare client relationships
- **Compensation**: 8-15% referral fees
- **Revenue Potential**: $200K-$1M annually per partner

#### Partner Types:
- **Healthcare Law Firms**: Legal advisors to healthcare organizations
- **Healthcare Accounting**: Accounting firms serving healthcare clients
- **Consulting Firms**: Healthcare consulting and advisory firms
- **Government Relations**: Healthcare government relations consultants

## Referral Process Framework

### Referral Identification

#### Lead Qualification Criteria
- **Healthcare Organization**: Hospital, health system, medical practice, or payer
- **Decision Authority**: Economic buyer with budget authority and decision-making power
- **Qualified Need**: Clear business need matching our solution capabilities
- **Timeline**: 6-12 month buying cycle with budgeted initiatives

#### Referral Quality Scoring
- **Partner Relationship Quality** (30%): Strength and depth of partner relationship
- **Lead Quality Score** (25%): Lead fit, authority, need, and timeline
- **Revenue Potential** (25%): Deal size and revenue potential
- **Strategic Value** (20%): Market importance, reference value, expansion potential

### Referral Management Process

#### Step 1: Referral Submission
- **Simple Submission**: Online referral submission form with minimal friction
- **Lead Information**: Basic lead information, contact details, and opportunity description
- **Partner Relationship**: Partner's relationship to the prospect and level of influence
- **Deal Context**: Deal context, timeline, budget, and competitive situation

#### Step 2: Lead Qualification (24-48 hours)
- **Internal Qualification**: Sales team qualification within 48 hours
- **Partner Notification**: Automatic notification to referring partner of qualification status
- **Additional Information**: Request for additional information if needed
- **Next Steps**: Clear next steps and timeline for partner and prospect

#### Step 3: Sales Engagement (1-3 days)
- **Initial Contact**: Sales team makes initial contact within 3 business days
- **Partner Involvement**: Partner involved in initial meetings as appropriate
- **Opportunity Development**: Full opportunity development and qualification process
- **Regular Updates**: Regular updates to referring partner throughout sales process

#### Step 4: Deal Tracking and Reporting
- **Pipeline Tracking**: Full pipeline tracking with partner visibility
- **Status Updates**: Regular status updates to referring partner
- **Deal Close**: Partner notification and involvement in deal close process
- **Commission Calculation**: Automated commission calculation and payment processing

### Referral Protection and Governance

#### Deal Registration and Protection
- **Time-Based Protection**: 180-day protection period from referral submission
- **Exclusivity**: Exclusive referral credit for qualified referrals
- **Conflict Resolution**: Clear process for resolving referral conflicts
- **Documentation**: Comprehensive documentation of referral relationships

#### Quality Assurance
- **Referral Quality**: Regular review of referral quality and partner performance
- **Partner Feedback**: Regular feedback on referral quality and process
- **Process Improvement**: Continuous process improvement based on partner feedback
- **Performance Management**: Performance management and optimization
