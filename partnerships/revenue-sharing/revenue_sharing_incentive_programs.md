# Healthcare Partnership Revenue Sharing and Incentive Programs

## Program Overview

The Revenue Sharing and Incentive Programs are designed to create mutually beneficial financial structures that align partner interests with business objectives while providing attractive compensation models that reward performance and drive long-term partnership success.

## Revenue Sharing Framework

### Tiered Revenue Sharing Structure

#### Strategic Technology Partners
**Epic, Cerner, Allscripts, MEDITECH Partnerships**

##### Platinum Tier Revenue Share
**Qualification**: Strategic partnership with $500K+ annual investment
**Base Revenue Share**: 15-20% of net new revenue
**Performance Bonuses**: Up to 5% additional for performance targets
**Long-term Incentives**: Additional 2-3% for multi-year commitments

**Revenue Share Calculation**:
- **Tier 1**: $0-$2M annual revenue = 15% revenue share
- **Tier 2**: $2M-$5M annual revenue = 17% revenue share
- **Tier 3**: $5M-$10M annual revenue = 20% revenue share
- **Tier 4**: $10M+ annual revenue = 22% revenue share

**Performance Bonuses**:
- **120% of Target**: +2% bonus revenue share
- **150% of Target**: +3% bonus revenue share
- **180% of Target**: +5% bonus revenue share
- **Innovation Bonus**: +1-2% for joint innovation initiatives

##### Gold Tier Revenue Share
**Qualification**: Gold partnership with $250K-$500K annual investment
**Base Revenue Share**: 12-18% of net new revenue
**Performance Bonuses**: Up to 3% additional for performance targets

**Revenue Share Calculation**:
- **Tier 1**: $0-$1M annual revenue = 12% revenue share
- **Tier 2**: $1M-$3M annual revenue = 15% revenue share
- **Tier 3**: $3M-$5M annual revenue = 18% revenue share

#### Channel Partners and System Integrators
**National, Regional, and Specialized Integrators**

##### Channel Partner Revenue Share Structure
**Reseller Discount Model**:
- **Platinum Partners**: 25% base discount + 5% performance bonus
- **Gold Partners**: 20% base discount + 3% performance bonus
- **Silver Partners**: 15% base discount + 2% performance bonus

**Implementation Revenue Share**:
- **Service Revenue**: 20-30% of implementation service revenue
- **Training Revenue**: 25-35% of training and certification revenue
- **Support Revenue**: 15-25% of ongoing support revenue
- **Custom Development**: 10-20% of custom development revenue

**Recurring Revenue Sharing**:
- **Annual Recurring Revenue**: 10-15% of ARR for first 3 years
- **Maintenance Revenue**: 20-25% of maintenance revenue
- **Upgrade Revenue**: 15-20% of software upgrade revenue
- **Additional Module Revenue**: 10-15% of additional module revenue

##### Deal-Based Revenue Sharing
**New Customer Acquisition**:
- **Finder's Fee**: 8-12% for qualified introductions
- **Active Sales Participation**: 15-20% for active sales involvement
- **Implementation Leadership**: 5-8% for implementation leadership
- **Customer Success**: 3-5% for customer success responsibility

**Existing Customer Expansion**:
- **Upselling**: 10-15% for successful upselling initiatives
- **Cross-selling**: 8-12% for successful cross-selling
- **Renewals**: 5-8% for renewal facilitation and support
- **Reference Value**: 2-3% for reference and case study participation

## Performance-Based Compensation

### Key Performance Indicators (KPIs)

#### Revenue Metrics (40% Weight)
**Primary Revenue KPIs**:
- **Net New Revenue**: Revenue from new customer acquisitions
- **Expansion Revenue**: Revenue from existing customer expansion
- **Recurring Revenue**: Annual recurring revenue contribution
- **Total Revenue**: Total revenue contribution and growth

**Revenue Performance Tiers**:
- **Exceeds Expectations**: 120%+ of target = Maximum compensation
- **Meets Expectations**: 100-119% of target = Standard compensation
- **Approaching Expectations**: 80-99% of target = Partial compensation
- **Below Expectations**: <80% of target = Base compensation only

#### Operational Metrics (30% Weight)
**Primary Operational KPIs**:
- **Sales Cycle**: Average sales cycle time and improvement
- **Win Rate**: Deal win rate and competitive performance
- **Customer Satisfaction**: Customer satisfaction scores and feedback
- **Implementation Success**: Implementation success rates and timing

**Operational Performance Standards**:
- **Sales Cycle**: 10-20% improvement year-over-year
- **Win Rate**: 15-25% win rate with continuous improvement
- **Customer Satisfaction**: 90%+ customer satisfaction scores
- **Implementation Success**: 95%+ implementation success rates

#### Strategic Metrics (20% Weight)
**Primary Strategic KPIs**:
- **Market Penetration**: Market penetration and share growth
- **Brand Recognition**: Brand recognition and awareness improvement
- **Innovation Index**: Innovation contributions and new solution development
- **Partnership Quality**: Partnership quality and relationship strength

**Strategic Performance Indicators**:
- **Market Share**: Measurable market share improvement
- **Brand Value**: Brand value and recognition enhancement
- **Innovation Output**: Innovation output and patent development
- **Relationship Score**: Partnership relationship quality scores

#### Customer Impact Metrics (10% Weight)
**Primary Customer KPIs**:
- **Customer Retention**: Customer retention and renewal rates
- **Customer Expansion**: Customer expansion and upselling success
- **Reference Value**: Customer reference value and case studies
- **Advocacy**: Customer advocacy and referral generation
