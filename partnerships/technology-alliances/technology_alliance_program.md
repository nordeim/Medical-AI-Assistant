# Healthcare Technology Alliance Partnership Program

## Program Overview

The Technology Alliance Partnership Program focuses on building deep technical integrations and strategic alliances with Electronic Health Record (EHR) and Electronic Medical Record (EMR) vendors, healthcare technology platforms, and complementary healthcare solutions. These partnerships emphasize technical interoperability, joint solution development, and integrated go-to-market strategies.
