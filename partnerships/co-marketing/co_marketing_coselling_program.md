# Healthcare Co-Marketing and Co-Selling Program

## Program Overview

The Co-Marketing and Co-Selling Program creates mutually beneficial partnerships that combine marketing resources, sales efforts, and market intelligence to accelerate growth and market penetration in healthcare markets. This program focuses on joint go-to-market strategies, shared marketing initiatives, and collaborative sales execution.
