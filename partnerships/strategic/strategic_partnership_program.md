## Partnership Framework

### Strategic Partnership Tiers

#### Platinum Partners ($500K+ annual investment)
- **Benefits**: Joint solution development, executive sponsorship, dedicated resources
- **Commitments**: Annual revenue targets, joint marketing, technical integration
- **Support**: Executive business reviews, priority technical support, co-selling teams

#### Gold Partners ($250K-$500K annual investment)
- **Benefits**: Integration support, co-marketing, sales enablement
- **Commitments**: Revenue targets, marketing collaboration, technical cooperation
- **Support**: Quarterly reviews, technical support, sales training

#### Silver Partners ($100K-$250K annual investment)
- **Benefits**: Integration tools, marketing support, certification programs
- **Commitments**: Minimum revenue commitments, certification completion
- **Support**: Standard support, online resources, annual reviews

### Partner Evaluation Criteria

#### Strategic Fit (40%)
- Market presence and reputation
- Customer base alignment
- Product complementary fit
- Geographic coverage

#### Technical Compatibility (30%)
- API availability and quality
- Integration complexity
- Security and compliance standards
- Scalability considerations

#### Commercial Viability (20%)
- Revenue potential
- Investment requirements
- Time to market
- Competitive landscape

#### Operational Capability (10%)
- Implementation support
- Partner management maturity
- Training and certification programs
- Support infrastructure

## Risk Management

### Key Risks
1. **Partner Conflicts**: Disagreements over territory or customers
2. **Technical Challenges**: Integration complexity or performance issues
3. **Market Changes**: Changes in partner strategy or market conditions
4. **Competitive Pressure**: Competitor partnerships or direct competition

### Mitigation Strategies
- **Clear Agreements**: Comprehensive partnership agreements with exit clauses
- **Regular Communication**: Frequent check-ins and relationship management
- **Alternative Partners**: Multiple partner options for key capabilities
- **Performance Monitoring**: Regular performance reviews and optimization
