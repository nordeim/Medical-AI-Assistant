# Healthcare Channel Partner Program

## Program Overview

The Healthcare Channel Partner Program targets system integrators, consulting firms, and IT service providers who implement and support healthcare technology solutions. This program provides structure for partners to resell, implement, and support our healthcare solutions.

## Target Channel Partners

### Tier 1 System Integrators (National Reach)
**Target Partners**: Accenture, Deloitte, IBM, Capgemini, Wipro, Cognizant
- **Market Coverage**: National healthcare markets
- **Focus Areas**: Large health systems, academic medical centers
- **Investment Level**: $300K-$500K annually per partner
- **Revenue Potential**: $5M-$15M annually per partner

### Tier 2 Regional Integrators
**Target Partners**: NTT Data, CGI, Leidos, SAIC, HCL Technologies
- **Market Coverage**: Regional healthcare markets
- **Focus Areas**: Mid-size health systems, specialty practices
- **Investment Level**: $150K-$300K annually per partner
- **Revenue Potential**: $2M-$8M annually per partner

### Tier 3 Specialized Partners
**Target Partners**: Healthcare-focused consultancies and boutique integrators
- **Market Coverage**: Niche markets and specialties
- **Focus Areas**: Specific healthcare verticals, rural markets
- **Investment Level**: $50K-$150K annually per partner
- **Revenue Potential**: $500K-$3M annually per partner

## Channel Program Structure

### Platinum Channel Partners
**Qualification Requirements**:
- $10M+ annual healthcare IT revenue
- 50+ healthcare technology professionals
- National or multi-regional presence
- Healthcare compliance certifications

**Benefits and Incentives**:
- 25% base reseller discount
- Joint go-to-market funding (up to $200K annually)
- Priority technical support and escalation
- Executive relationship management
- Co-selling resources and deal registration

**Commitments**:
- Minimum $5M annual revenue commitment
- 20+ certified professionals
- Quarterly business reviews
- Joint marketing activities

### Gold Channel Partners
**Qualification Requirements**:
- $2M+ annual healthcare IT revenue
- 20+ healthcare technology professionals
- Regional market presence
- Relevant certifications

**Benefits and Incentives**:
- 20% base reseller discount
- Co-marketing support (up to $100K annually)
- Standard technical support
- Channel account management
- Sales training and enablement

**Commitments**:
- Minimum $2M annual revenue commitment
- 10+ certified professionals
- Semi-annual business reviews
- Marketing collaboration

### Silver Channel Partners
**Qualification Requirements**:
- $500K+ annual healthcare IT revenue
- 5+ healthcare technology professionals
- Local or niche market focus
- Basic certifications

**Benefits and Incentives**:
- 15% base reseller discount
- Marketing development funds (up to $25K annually)
- Standard support access
- Online training resources
- Partner portal access

**Commitments**:
- Minimum $500K annual revenue commitment
- 3+ certified professionals
- Annual business reviews
- Basic marketing activities

## Partner Recruitment Strategy

### Recruitment Criteria

#### Financial Capability
- **Revenue Requirements**: Minimum $500K annual healthcare IT revenue
- **Growth Trajectory**: 20%+ year-over-year growth
- **Financial Stability**: Positive cash flow and profitability
- **Investment Capacity**: Ability to invest in training and certifications

#### Technical Expertise
- **Healthcare IT Experience**: 5+ years in healthcare technology
- **Technical Certifications**: Relevant vendor certifications
- **Implementation Experience**: Proven track record of implementations
- **Support Capabilities**: 24/7 support capability preferred

#### Market Presence
- **Customer Base**: Established healthcare customer relationships
- **Geographic Coverage**: Clear market coverage strategy
- **Industry Reputation**: Positive references and market reputation
- **Competitive Position**: Strong competitive positioning

### Recruitment Process

#### Phase 1: Partner Identification
- **Market Research**: Identify potential partners through market analysis
- **Competitive Analysis**: Assess partner relationships with competitors
- **Reference Checks**: Validate partner capabilities through references
- **Initial Outreach**: Contact potential partners through executive relationships

#### Phase 2: Qualification Assessment
- **Financial Review**: Assess financial stability and capabilities
- **Technical Evaluation**: Review technical expertise and certifications
- **Customer Validation**: Interview existing customers about partner performance
- **Strategic Alignment**: Evaluate strategic fit and partnership potential

#### Phase 3: Partnership Negotiation
- **Term Sheet**: Develop mutual term sheet outlining partnership terms
- **Contract Negotiation**: Negotiate partnership agreement and terms
- **Resource Planning**: Plan initial resource allocation and support
- **Launch Planning**: Develop partner launch timeline and activities

#### Phase 4: Onboarding and Launch
- **Contract Execution**: Finalize and execute partnership agreements
- **Team Onboarding**: Train partner teams on products and processes
- **System Setup**: Set up partner systems, access, and tools
- **Go-to-Market Launch**: Launch partnership with joint marketing
