## Resource Requirements

### Human Resources
- Project management office: 3-5 FTEs
- Subject matter experts: 10-15 FTEs
- Training and development team: 5-8 FTEs
- Change management support: 2-3 FTEs
- Analytics and reporting team: 3-5 FTEs

### Technology Investment
- Learning management system: $200K-$500K
- Talent management platform: $300K-$700K
- Analytics and reporting tools: $100K-$300K
- Communication and collaboration tools: $50K-$150K
- Integration and development: $200K-$400K

### Training & Development
- Leadership development programs: $300K-$500K

- External consulting and expertise: $200K-$400K
- Conference and professional development: $100K-$200K
- Technology training and support: $150K-$250K

## Risk Management & Mitigation

### High-Priority Risks
1. **Leadership Commitment Fading**
   - Mitigation: Regular executive engagement and reporting
   - Contingency: Alternative sponsorship and advocacy

2. **Budget Constraints**
   - Mitigation: ROI-focused implementation and prioritization
   - Contingency: Phased implementation and resource reallocation

3. **Change Resistance**
   - Mitigation: Comprehensive change management and communication
   - Contingency: Additional training and support resources

4. **Technology Integration Issues**
   - Mitigation: Thorough planning and testing protocols
   - Contingency: Alternative solutions and workarounds

5. **Talent Acquisition Challenges**
   - Mitigation: Competitive compensation and employer branding
   - Contingency: External resource augmentation and consulting
