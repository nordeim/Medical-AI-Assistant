# Talent Management Success Metrics & KPI Dashboard

## Strategic Metrics Overview

This comprehensive metrics framework provides quantitative and qualitative measures to track the success and effectiveness of all talent management initiatives across the seven strategic pillars.

## 1. Talent Acquisition & Retention Metrics

### Recruitment Effectiveness
- **Time to Hire**: Target <30 days, Industry benchmark: 45 days
- **Cost per Hire**: Reduce by 20% annually
- **Quality of Hire Score**: >85% (6-month performance, retention, and cultural fit)
- **Offer Acceptance Rate**: >90% (target 95%)
- **Source Quality Score**: Track by recruitment channel
- **Candidate Experience Rating**: >4.5/5.0

### Retention Excellence
- **Voluntary Turnover Rate**: <10% annually
- **First-Year Retention Rate**: >95%
- **Regrettable Attrition Rate**: <5%
- **Internal Mobility Rate**: >15% of workforce annually
- **Retirement and Succession Transition**: 100% planned transitions
- **Exit Interview Action Rate**: >80% of actionable insights implemented
