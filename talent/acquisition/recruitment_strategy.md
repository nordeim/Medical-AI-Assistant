# Talent Acquisition & Retention Optimization Strategy

A comprehensive talent acquisition and retention framework designed to attract top-tier talent and create an environment where employees thrive and stay committed to organizational success.

**Digital Talent Platforms**
- AI-powered candidate matching systems
- Professional networking optimization (LinkedIn, GitHub, Dribbble)
- Social media recruitment campaigns
- Employee referral program enhancement

**Traditional & Niche Channels**
- University partnerships and campus recruiting
- Industry conference and event participation
- Professional association memberships
- Military and veteran transition programs

**Internal Talent Mobility**
- Internal job board and career pathing
- Cross-functional rotation programs
- Internal talent marketplace
- Skills-based internal mobility

- Company culture videos and testimonials
- Accessible application process

**Selection Process**
- Structured interview frameworks
- Skills assessments and work samples
- Cultural fit evaluation
- Reference and background checks

**Offer & Negotiation**
- Competitive compensation packages
- Flexible benefits selection
- Signing bonus and relocation support
- Career development roadmap
