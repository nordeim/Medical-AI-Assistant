# Patient Privacy and Safety Guide

## Understanding Your Privacy Rights

### What Information We Collect
During your consultation with the Medical AI Assistant, we may collect:

#### Health Information
- **Symptoms**: Information about health problems you're experiencing
- **Medical History**: Background information about your health conditions
- **Current Medications**: Medications you're currently taking
- **Allergies**: Known allergies or adverse reactions

#### Technical Information
- **Device Information**: Type of device and browser you're using
- **Internet Connection**: Basic connection information for security
- **Usage Patterns**: How you interact with the system (for improvement purposes)

#### What We DON'T Collect
- **Personal Identifiers**: Name, address, phone number, email
- **Social Security Number**: Never asked for or stored
- **Financial Information**: Insurance details, payment information
- **Identity Information**: Photos, biometric data, or identity documents

## How We Protect Your Information

### Technical Safeguards
- **Encryption**: All data is encrypted both during transmission and storage
- **Access Controls**: Only authorized healthcare professionals can access your information
- **Audit Trails**: All access to your information is logged and monitored
- **Secure Servers**: Data is stored on secure, HIPAA-compliant servers

### Physical Safeguards
- **Controlled Access**: Server facilities have physical access controls
- **Monitored Facilities**: 24/7 security monitoring of data centers
- **Backup Security**: Encrypted backups stored in secure locations

### Administrative Safeguards
- **Staff Training**: All staff receive regular privacy and security training
- **Access Procedures**: Strict procedures for who can access patient information
- **Incident Response**: Plans in place for any security incidents
- **Regular Audits**: Regular reviews of privacy and security practices

## HIPAA Compliance

### Your HIPAA Rights
As a patient, you have the following rights under HIPAA:

#### Right to Access
- **View Your Records**: You can request to see the information we have about your consultation
- **Copy Records**: You can request copies of your medical records
- **Electronic Copies**: You can request electronic copies of your records

#### Right to Request Amendments
- **Correct Errors**: You can request that we correct any information you believe is incorrect
- **Add Information**: You can request that we add information you believe is missing
- **Response Timeline**: We must respond to your request within 60 days

#### Right to Restrict Information Use
- **Limit Sharing**: You can request limits on how your information is used or shared
- **Restrict Access**: You can request that certain people not have access to your information
- **Note Restrictions**: We will note any restrictions in your medical record

#### Right to File Complaints
- **File with Us**: You can file a complaint with us about privacy concerns
- **File with OCR**: You can file a complaint with the Office for Civil Rights
- **No Retaliation**: You will not be retaliated against for filing complaints

### Our HIPAA Responsibilities
- **Maintain Privacy**: We must maintain the privacy of your protected health information
- **Provide Rights**: We must provide you with information about your rights
- **Breach Notification**: We must notify you of any breach of your protected health information
- **Business Associates**: We must ensure that any third parties handling your information also protect it

## Data Retention and Deletion

### How Long We Keep Information
- **Consultation Data**: Kept for 7 years as required by healthcare regulations
- **System Logs**: Kept for 1 year for security and system improvement
- **Aggregated Data**: De-identified data may be kept indefinitely for research and improvement

### How You Can Delete Information
- **Request Deletion**: You can request that your personal consultation data be deleted
- **Partial Deletion**: You can request deletion of specific portions of your data
- **Timing**: We will respond to deletion requests within 30 days
- **Exceptions**: Some information must be retained for legal and regulatory requirements

## Third-Party Sharing

### When We Share Your Information
We may share your information in the following circumstances:

#### With Your Healthcare Team
- **Primary Care Provider**: Your primary doctor or clinic
- **Specialists**: Any specialists involved in your care
- **Care Coordinators**: Staff coordinating your care

#### For Legal Requirements
- **Law Enforcement**: When required by law enforcement with proper legal process
- **Court Orders**: When required by court order
- **Public Health**: For public health reporting as required by law
- **Safety Issues**: When there is a serious threat to safety

### When We DON'T Share Your Information
- **Marketing**: We never share your information for marketing purposes
- **Commercial Purposes**: We don't sell or rent your information
- **Social Media**: We don't share information on social media
- **Research Without Consent**: We don't use your information for research without your explicit consent

## Security Best Practices

### What You Can Do to Protect Your Privacy
- **Private Environment**: Use the system in a private location
- **Secure Devices**: Use secure, password-protected devices
- **Secure Networks**: Avoid public Wi-Fi when possible
- **Log Out**: Always log out when finished with your consultation
- **Report Issues**: Report any privacy or security concerns immediately

### Warning Signs of Privacy Issues
- **Unexpected Communications**: Contact you didn't expect
- **Suspicious Requests**: Requests for information we wouldn't normally ask for
- **System Errors**: Repeated system errors or unusual behavior
- **Identity Theft**: Signs that someone else may be using your identity

## Frequently Asked Privacy Questions

### Q: Who can see my consultation information?
**A:** Only authorized healthcare professionals involved in your care can access your information. All access is logged and monitored.

### Q: Can my employer see my consultation information?
**A:** No. Your consultation information is protected health information and cannot be shared with employers without your explicit consent.

### Q: What happens if there's a data breach?
**A:** We will notify you immediately if there's a breach affecting your information, investigate the incident, and take steps to prevent similar incidents.

### Q: Can I use this system if I don't have health insurance?
**A:** Yes. Insurance status does not affect your ability to use the system or your privacy rights.

### Q: How do I know if my information is secure?
**A:** We use industry-standard security measures and undergo regular security audits. You can also take steps to protect your privacy as outlined in this guide.
