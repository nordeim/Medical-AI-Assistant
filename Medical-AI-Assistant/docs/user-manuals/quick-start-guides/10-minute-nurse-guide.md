# 10-Minute Nurse Quick Start Guide

## üöÄ Start Using the System in 10 Minutes

### Step 1: Access Your Dashboard (2 minutes)
1. **Login**: Use your secure credentials to access the nurse dashboard
2. **Complete Training**: Ensure you've finished required training modules
3. **Check Queue**: Review the pending assessments queue

### Step 2: Review Your First Assessment (4 minutes)
1. **Select Assessment**: Click on the first pending assessment in your queue
2. **Read AI Summary**: Review the AI-generated patient summary:
   - Patient age group and chief complaint
   - Symptoms and medical history
   - AI risk assessment and recommendations
3. **Review Conversation**: Read through the patient-AI conversation transcript

### Step 3: Apply Clinical Judgment (3 minutes)
Ask yourself:
- Does the AI assessment align with your clinical knowledge?
- Are there any red flags that need immediate attention?
- What would your independent clinical assessment be?
- Should you accept, modify, or escalate the AI recommendations?

### Step 4: Make Your Decision (1 minute)
Choose your action:
- **Accept AI**: If recommendations align with your clinical judgment
- **Modify**: Adjust triage level or recommendations based on your expertise
- **Escalate**: Send to physician for further evaluation
- **Request Info**: Ask for additional patient information

### Emergency Recognition
**AI Emergency Flags:**
- Chest pain or cardiac symptoms
- Respiratory distress
- Stroke symptoms (facial drooping, arm weakness, speech difficulty)
- Severe bleeding or shock symptoms

**Remember:** Always verify emergencies independently - AI can miss subtle presentations.

### Making Clinical Decisions
**Decision Framework:**
1. **Trust Your Expertise**: Your clinical judgment supersedes AI recommendations
2. **Document Everything**: Record your reasoning and decisions
3. **Communicate Clearly**: Explain your decisions to patients and colleagues
4. **Follow Protocols**: Maintain established clinical protocols

## üö® Emergency Situations - Quick Response

### When AI Flags an Emergency:
1. **STOP**: Immediately stop normal assessment review
2. **VERIFY**: Independently verify emergency status
3. **CONTACT**: Attempt direct patient contact if possible
4. **ACT**: Make immediate clinical decisions
5. **DOCUMENT**: Record entire emergency response process

### When You Detect an Emergency:
- Follow your standard emergency protocols
- Don't wait for AI system to catch up
- Trust your clinical instincts
- Contact emergency services as needed
- Notify supervising physician

## ‚ùì Quick Answers to Common Questions

**Q: What if I disagree with AI recommendations?**
A: Always trust your clinical judgment. You can modify or override any AI recommendation. Document your reasoning.

**Q: How do I handle incomplete assessments?**
A: Use the "Request More Information" feature to get additional details from patients.

**Q: What if I can't reach a patient?**
A: Make decisions based on available information and follow your organization's protocols for unreachable patients.

**Q: How do I prioritize the queue?**
A: Focus on emergencies first, then urgent cases, then routine cases. Use your clinical judgment for prioritization.

**Q: What if the system isn't working?**
A: Contact technical support immediately and follow backup documentation procedures.
