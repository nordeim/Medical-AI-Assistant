## ðŸ’° Pricing Models

### 1. Subscription Model
- **Base Pricing**: $120,000 - $500,000 annually based on market segment
- **Volume Discounts**: 5-20% based on patient volume tiers
- **Technology Adoption**: Premium/discount based on adoption score
- **Tier Multipliers**: Bronze (0.7x) to Platinum (2.0x)

### 2. Enterprise License
- **Setup Fee**: 20% of annual license fee
- **Annual Maintenance**: 15% of license fee
- **Implementation**: Custom based on complexity

### 3. Value-Based Pricing
- **Value Capture Rate**: 20-30% of demonstrated value
- **Outcome Guarantees**: Tiered pricing based on confidence levels
- **Performance Metrics**: Clinical outcomes, operational efficiency, financial impact
- **Measurement Period**: 12-36 months with periodic adjustments

### 4. Per-Patient Pricing
- **Base Rate**: $0.50 per patient encounter
- **Complexity Multipliers**: 1.2x - 2.0x based on clinical specialty
- **Volume Commitments**: Annual minimums with overage rates
- **Service Level**: Standard to premium support tiers
