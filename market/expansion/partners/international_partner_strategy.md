## Partner Program Framework

### Partner Certification Levels

#### Platinum Partners
- **Requirements**: $10M+ annual revenue, certified team, 50+ implementations
- **Benefits**: 
  - Joint go-to-market planning
  - Priority technical support
  - Co-marketing budget: $500K-$1M
  - Executive engagement programs
- **Margins**: 25-30%
- **Investment**: $5-10M per partner

#### Gold Partners
- **Requirements**: $2M+ annual revenue, certified team, 20+ implementations
- **Benefits**:
  - Sales and technical training
  - Lead generation support
  - Co-marketing budget: $100K-$500K
  - Quarterly business reviews
- **Margins**: 20-25%
- **Investment**: $1-3M per partner

#### Silver Partners
- **Requirements**: $500K+ annual revenue, basic certification, 5+ implementations
- **Benefits**:
  - Standard training programs
  - Partner portal access
  - Basic co-marketing support
  - Online training resources
- **Margins**: 15-20%
- **Investment**: $100K-$500K per partner

#### Authorized Partners
- **Requirements**: Basic certification, commitment to learning
- **Benefits**:
  - Online training and certification
  - Partner resource center access
  - Basic technical support
  - Marketing materials
- **Margins**: 10-15%
- **Investment**: $25K-$100K per partner

## Risk Management

### Partnership Risks
1. **Partner Performance**: Poor execution or customer satisfaction
   - Mitigation: Rigorous certification and performance monitoring
   - Response: Performance improvement plans or termination

2. **Channel Conflict**: Direct vs. partner sales conflicts
   - Mitigation: Clear rules of engagement and deal registration
   - Response: Regular conflict resolution processes

3. **Partner Dependency**: Over-reliance on key partners
   - Mitigation: Diversified partner portfolio
   - Response: Direct sales capability development

### Market Risks
1. **Regulatory Changes**: Changes in healthcare regulations
   - Mitigation: Continuous regulatory monitoring
   - Response: Rapid compliance adaptation

2. **Competitive Pressure**: Aggressive partner recruitment by competitors
   - Mitigation: Unique value proposition and investment
   - Response: Enhanced partner programs and retention

3. **Economic Downturns**: Healthcare budget constraints
   - Mitigation: Flexible partnership terms
   - Response: Cost-justified solutions and ROI focus
