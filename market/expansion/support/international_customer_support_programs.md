  - Low (P4): 48 hours
- **Resolution Target**: 90% within 48 hours
- **Availability**: 24/7/365

#### Tier 3 Support (Engineering)
- **Response Time**:
  - Critical (P1): 15 minutes
  - High (P2): 1 hour
  - Medium (P3): 4 hours
  - Low (P4): 24 hours
- **Resolution Target**: 95% within SLA
- **Availability**: 24/7 for P1/P2, business hours for P3/P4

### Priority Classification

#### Critical Issues (P1)
- **Definition**: System completely down, no workaround available
- **Examples**:
  - Complete system failure
  - Data corruption or loss
  - Security breach or vulnerability
  - Regulatory compliance violation
- **Impact**: No clinical operations possible
- **Customer Notification**: Immediate escalation notification

#### High Priority Issues (P2)
- **Definition**: Major functionality impaired, significant workaround
- **Examples**:
  - Key feature not working
  - Performance degradation affecting clinical workflow
  - Integration failures
  - Multiple user impact
- **Impact**: Significant operational impact
- **Customer Notification**: Within 30 minutes

#### Medium Priority Issues (P3)
- **Definition**: Minor functionality impaired, easy workaround
- **Examples**:
  - Cosmetic issues
  - Non-critical feature requests
  - Single user impact
  - Documentation updates needed
- **Impact**: Minimal operational impact
- **Customer Notification**: Within 4 hours

#### Low Priority Issues (P4)
- **Definition**: General inquiries, enhancement requests
- **Examples**:
  - General questions
  - Feature requests
  - Training requests
  - Best practice consultations
- **Impact**: No operational impact
- **Customer Notification**: Within 24 hours

### Service Level Guarantees

#### Uptime Guarantees
- **Platform Uptime**: 99.9% monthly uptime guarantee
- **Critical Systems**: 99.99% uptime guarantee
- **Planned Maintenance**: <4 hours monthly, announced 2 weeks prior
- **Credits**: Service credits for uptime violations

#### Response Time Guarantees
- **Priority Response**: Meet SLA targets >95% of the time
- **Resolution Time**: Meet resolution targets >90% of the time
- **Escalation**: Automatic escalation for SLA violations
- **Credits**: Service credits for SLA violations

#### Quality Guarantees

- **Customer Satisfaction**: >4.5/5.0 satisfaction rating
