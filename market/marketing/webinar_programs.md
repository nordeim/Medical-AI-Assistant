### Lead Generation and Qualification

#### Registration Form Strategy
**Required Information:**
- Name, email, job title
- Healthcare organization name
- Organization size (number of employees/beds)
- Current AI adoption status
- Primary area of interest

**Progressive Profiling:**
- First webinar: Basic contact information
- Subsequent webinars: Additional qualification questions
- Nurture sequence: Detailed needs assessment

#### Lead Scoring Criteria
**High-Quality Leads (Score 80+):**
- C-level executive or medical director
- Organization with 500+ employees
- Currently evaluating AI solutions
- Engaged with multiple content pieces
- Requested demo or consultation

**Medium-Quality Leads (Score 50-79):**
- Director-level healthcare professional
- Organization with 100-500 employees
- Interest in AI but no active evaluation
- Engaged with content but no direct contact

**Low-Quality Leads (Score 20-49):**
- Manager or individual contributor level
- Small healthcare organization (<100 employees)
- General interest in healthcare AI
- Minimal engagement history

#### Lead Nurturing Workflows

**High-Quality Lead Sequence:**
- Immediate follow-up email (same day)
- Personalized outreach call (within 24 hours)
- Custom ROI analysis offer (within 48 hours)
- Executive briefing invitation (within 1 week)
- Demo scheduling (within 2 weeks)

**Medium-Quality Lead Sequence:**
- Thank you email with resources (same day)
- Educational content series (weekly for 4 weeks)
- Industry report offer (week 2)
- Webinar invitation for next topic (week 3)
- Sales team qualification call (week 4)
