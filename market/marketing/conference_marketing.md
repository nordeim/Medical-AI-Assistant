### Pre-Conference Marketing Strategy

#### 90 Days Before Conference
**Strategic Planning Phase:**
- Conference objectives and success metrics definition
- Booth design and logistics coordination
- Speaking proposal submissions
- Media partnership negotiations
- Staff training and preparation planning

#### 60 Days Before Conference
**Marketing Campaign Launch:**
- **Email Campaigns:** Targeted outreach to conference attendee lists
- **Social Media Campaign:** Conference-specific hashtag and content strategy
- **Content Development:** Booth presentation materials and demo scripts
- **Partnership Activation:** Collaborate with conference organizers and sponsors
- **Media Relations:** Press release and media kit preparation

#### 30 Days Before Conference
**Aggressive Promotion Phase:**
- **Last-Chance Registration:** Limited-time offers and incentives
- **Social Media Countdown:** Daily countdown with booth location and highlights
- **Influencer Outreach:** Key opinion leader engagement and booth visits
- **Staff Preparation:** Final training, role assignments, and logistics
- **Technology Testing:** All booth technology and demo testing

#### 7 Days Before Conference
**Final Preparations:**
- **Attendee List Analysis:** Identify and prioritize key prospects
- **Meeting Scheduling:** Book private demos and consultations
- **Media Interviews:** Schedule press interviews and media coverage
- **Logistics Confirmation:** Shipping, setup, and support arrangements
- **Final Team Briefings:** Strategy review and role assignments

### Risk Management and Contingency Planning

#### Common Conference Challenges
**Low Attendance Risk:**
- **Mitigation:** Aggressive pre-conference promotion and incentives
- **Backup Plan:** Shift focus to quality over quantity with VIP targeting

**Technology Failures:**
- **Mitigation:** Redundant systems and backup equipment
- **Backup Plan:** Pre-recorded demos and offline interactive experiences

**Competitive Response:**
- **Mitigation:** Unique value proposition and differentiated experiences
- **Backup Plan:** Enhanced relationship building and thought leadership

**Economic Downturn Impact:**
- **Mitigation:** Flexible budget allocation and priority-based spending
- **Backup Plan:** Virtual conference alternatives and digital engagement
