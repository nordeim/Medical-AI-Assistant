# Healthcare Lead Scoring and Qualification System

## Executive Summary

This comprehensive lead scoring and qualification system is specifically designed for medical organizations and healthcare enterprises, incorporating industry-specific criteria, regulatory considerations, and complex decision-making structures typical in healthcare B2B sales.

## Healthcare Lead Scoring Framework

### Scoring Model Overview

The healthcare lead scoring system uses a 100-point scale with weighted categories that reflect the unique characteristics of healthcare organizations and their buying processes.

#### Scoring Categories
- **Company Profile (30 points)**: Organization size, type, and market position
- **Technology Readiness (25 points)**: Current technology stack and adoption
- **Financial Profile (20 points)**: Budget availability and investment capacity
- **Engagement Level (15 points)**: Interaction quality and interest indicators
- **Timing & Urgency (10 points)**: Decision timeline and need urgency

## Qualification Framework

### BANT + Healthcare Framework

#### Budget Qualification
```
Qualified Budget Indicators:
✅ Confirmed annual IT budget allocation
✅ Minimum $100K+ technology investment capacity
✅ Capital expenditure approval process defined
✅ ROI evaluation framework in place

Red Flags:
❌ No confirmed budget for projects
❌ Budget requests pending for >6 months
❌ Historical pattern of budget cuts
❌ Operating under financial constraints
```

#### Authority Qualification
```
Qualified Authority Access:
✅ Direct access to CIO/CTO or equivalent
✅ C-level sponsor identified and engaged
✅ Multi-level stakeholder support confirmed
✅ Committee decision-making process understood

Red Flags:
❌ Only departmental-level contacts
❌ No executive sponsorship
❌ Limited influence over decisions
❌ Unclear decision authority
```

#### Need Qualification
```
Qualified Need Indicators:
✅ Clear clinical or operational pain point
✅ Documented workflow inefficiencies
✅ Quantified improvement opportunities
✅ Stakeholder agreement on priorities

Red Flags:
❌ Vague or unclear requirements
❌ No identified pain points
❌ Feature requests without business case
❌ Competitive pressure without internal need
```

#### Timeline Qualification
```
Qualified Timeline:
✅ Implementation window confirmed
✅ Budget cycle alignment understood
✅ Decision process timeline mapped
✅ Resource availability confirmed

Red Flags:
❌ No specific implementation timeline
❌ Budget cycles not aligned
❌ Unclear decision process
❌ Resource constraints unknown
```

### Lead Scoring Thresholds

#### Hot Leads (80-100 points)
```
Characteristics:
- Immediate need confirmed
- Full stakeholder access
- Budget approved
- Integration feasible

Routing:
- Immediate assignment to senior AE
- Executive introduction within 24 hours
- Customized demo within 1 week
- POC planning initiated
```

#### Warm Leads (60-79 points)
```
Characteristics:
- Clear interest demonstrated
- Partial stakeholder access
- Budget likely available
- Technical requirements understood

Routing:
- Assignment to account executive
- Discovery call within 3 days
- Standard demo within 2 weeks
- Nurture program enrollment
```

#### Cold Leads (40-59 points)
```
Characteristics:
- Basic interest shown
- Limited stakeholder access
- Budget unconfirmed
- Early-stage evaluation

Routing:
- Assignment to inside sales
- Qualification call within 1 week
- Educational content delivery
- Nurture sequence activation
```

#### Unqualified Leads (<40 points)
```
Characteristics:
- Minimal engagement
- No clear need
- No stakeholder access
- No budget indication

Routing:
- Automated nurturing
- Educational content
- Quarterly follow-up
- Lead recycling after 6 months
```
