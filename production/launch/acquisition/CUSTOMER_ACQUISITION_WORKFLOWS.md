# Production Customer Acquisition Workflows

## Customer Acquisition Overview

### Target Customer Segments

#### Primary Segment: Healthcare Organizations (70% of target market)
1. **Large Hospital Systems (500+ beds)**
   - Academic medical centers
   - Regional healthcare networks
   - Multi-specialty hospital systems
   - **Target**: 50 organizations in first year
   - **Average Deal Size**: $250,000 - $2,000,000
   - **Sales Cycle**: 6-12 months

2. **Mid-Size Healthcare Organizations (100-500 beds)**
   - Community hospitals
   - Regional medical centers
   - Specialty clinic networks
   - **Target**: 100 organizations in first year
   - **Average Deal Size**: $75,000 - $250,000
   - **Sales Cycle**: 3-6 months

3. **Outpatient Care Centers**
   - Urgent care facilities
   - Ambulatory surgery centers
   - Multi-specialty clinics
   - **Target**: 200 organizations in first year
   - **Average Deal Size**: $25,000 - $75,000
   - **Sales Cycle**: 1-3 months

#### Secondary Segment: Healthcare Professionals (30% of target market)
1. **Individual Clinicians**
   - Primary care physicians
   - Specialists (cardiologists, oncologists, etc.)
   - Nurse practitioners
   - **Target**: 500 individual users in first year
   - **Average Deal Size**: $3,000 - $10,000
   - **Sales Cycle**: 1-2 weeks

2. **Small Practices (2-10 providers)**
   - Primary care practices
   - Specialty practices
   - Urgent care centers
   - **Target**: 150 practices in first year
   - **Average Deal Size**: $10,000 - $50,000
   - **Sales Cycle**: 2-4 weeks

## Lead Qualification Framework

### BANT Qualification Framework

#### Budget Qualification
- **Budget Availability**: Confirmed budget for AI/technology initiatives
- **Budget Range**: $25K - $2M+ annual technology spend
- **Decision Authority**: Access to budget approval process
- **Timeline**: 3-12 month implementation timeline

#### Authority Qualification
- **Decision Makers**: CMO, CIO, CMIO, CTO, VP Clinical Operations
- **Influencers**: Clinical directors, IT managers, department heads
- **Champion**: Internal advocate with budget and influence
- **Buying Committee**: Complete stakeholder mapping

#### Need Qualification
- **Pain Points**: Clinical workflow inefficiencies, documentation burden
- **Current Solutions**: Existing AI tools, manual processes, legacy systems
- **Business Drivers**: Cost reduction, quality improvement, compliance
- **Success Metrics**: Quantifiable improvement goals

#### Timeline Qualification
- **Implementation Window**: 3-12 month timeline
- **Decision Timeline**: 30-180 day decision process
- **Urgency Factors**: Regulatory deadlines, competitive pressure, ROI pressure
- **Project Dependencies**: EHR upgrades, infrastructure projects

#### Demographic Scoring (0-40 points)
- **Organization Size** (0-10 points)
  - 500+ beds: 10 points
  - 100-500 beds: 7 points
  - 50-100 beds: 5 points
  - 2-50 beds: 3 points
  - Individual providers: 1 point

- **Healthcare Segment** (0-10 points)
  - Hospital system: 10 points
  - Academic medical center: 8 points
  - Specialty clinic: 6 points
  - Primary care: 4 points
  - Other: 2 points

- **Geography** (0-10 points)
  - Target markets: 10 points
  - Secondary markets: 7 points
  - Tertiary markets: 5 points
  - International: 3 points

- **Technology Maturity** (0-10 points)
  - Advanced EHR (Epic, Cerner): 10 points
  - Mid-tier EHR: 7 points
  - Basic EHR: 5 points
  - Manual processes: 2 points

#### Behavioral Scoring (0-60 points)
- **Website Engagement** (0-20 points)
  - Demo request: 20 points
  - Whitepaper download: 15 points
  - Multiple page visits: 10 points
  - Single page visit: 5 points

- **Email Engagement** (0-20 points)
  - Replied to email: 20 points
  - Clicked multiple links: 15 points
  - Single link click: 10 points
  - Email open only: 5 points

- **Content Engagement** (0-20 points)
  - Webinar attendance: 20 points
  - Webinar registration: 15 points
  - Multiple content downloads: 12 points
  - Single content download: 8 points

### Lead Distribution Process

#### Automated Lead Routing
1. **Lead Assignment Rules**
   - Territory-based routing (North, South, East, West)
   - Segment-based routing (Enterprise, Mid-market, SMB)
   - Language-based routing (English, Spanish)
   - Specialty-based routing (cardiology, oncology, etc.)

2. **Lead Alert System**
   - High-priority leads (score 80+): Immediate alert to sales
   - Medium-priority leads (score 60-79): Same-day notification
   - Low-priority leads (score 40-59): Next-day notification
   - Nurture leads (score <40): Automated nurture sequence

3. **Sales Handoff Process**
   - Automated lead notification
   - CRM record creation with complete context
   - Lead scoring and prioritization
   - Follow-up task creation
   - Champion identification

## Sales Process Framework

### Enterprise Sales Process (>$100K deals)

#### Stage 1: Prospecting and Initial Contact (2-4 weeks)
**Activities:**
- Research target organization
- Identify key stakeholders
- Initial outreach (email + LinkedIn)
- Qualification discovery call
- **Exit Criteria**: BANT qualification confirmed

**Sales Materials:**
- Executive brief on healthcare AI value
- ROI calculator and business case
- Success story case studies
- Competitive differentiation guide

**Key Deliverables:**
- Customized business case presentation
- ROI analysis and cost justification
- Implementation timeline proposal
- Stakeholder mapping and engagement plan

#### Stage 2: Discovery and Needs Analysis (2-3 weeks)
**Activities:**
- Stakeholder interviews (clinical, IT, operations)
- Current state assessment
- Workflow mapping and analysis
- Pain point identification
- Success criteria definition
- **Exit Criteria**: Complete requirements documented

**Sales Materials:**
- Discovery questionnaire
- Current state assessment template
- Workflow optimization analysis
- Success criteria framework

**Key Deliverables:**
- Detailed requirements document
- Workflow optimization recommendations
- Implementation roadmap
- Success metrics definition

#### Stage 3: Solution Presentation and Demo (2-4 weeks)
**Activities:**
- Executive solution presentation
- Technical demonstration
- Clinical workflow walkthrough
- Integration planning session
- Security and compliance review
- **Exit Criteria**: Positive evaluation from key stakeholders

**Sales Materials:**
- Customized product demonstration
- Integration architecture proposal
- Security and compliance documentation
- Pilot program proposal

**Key Deliverables:**
- Proof of concept plan
- Technical integration proposal
- Security assessment report
- Pilot program framework

#### Stage 4: Proposal and Negotiation (2-3 weeks)
**Activities:**
- Custom proposal development
- Pricing negotiation
- Contract terms negotiation
- Legal and compliance review
- Implementation planning
- **Exit Criteria**: Proposal acceptance and contract signature

**Sales Materials:**
- Customized business proposal
- Pricing and packaging options
- Contract templates
- Implementation project plan

**Key Deliverables:**
- Signed master service agreement
- Statement of work
- Project timeline and milestones
- Implementation kickoff plan

### Mid-Market Sales Process ($25K-$100K deals)

#### Stage 1: Qualification and Discovery (1-2 weeks)
**Activities:**
- Initial qualification call
- Basic needs assessment
- Stakeholder identification
- Budget and timeline confirmation
- **Exit Criteria**: Clear need and timeline confirmed

#### Stage 2: Solution Presentation (1-2 weeks)
**Activities:**
- Product demonstration
- Business case presentation
- Implementation discussion
- Pricing proposal
- **Exit Criteria**: Positive response to proposal

#### Stage 3: Closing (1 week)
**Activities:**
- Contract negotiation
- Implementation planning
- Kickoff scheduling
- **Exit Criteria**: Contract signed and project initiated

### SMB Sales Process (<$25K deals)

#### Stage 1: Quick Qualification (3-5 days)
**Activities:**
- Initial call or demo request
- Basic needs assessment
- Budget confirmation
- Timeline establishment
- **Exit Criteria**: Need, budget, and timeline confirmed

#### Stage 2: Rapid Demo and Close (3-7 days)
**Activities:**
- Product demonstration
- Immediate proposal
- Quick close process
- Onboarding initiation
- **Exit Criteria**: Deal closed and implemented

## Customer Onboarding Workflow

### Enterprise Onboarding Process (8-12 weeks)

#### Phase 1: Project Initiation (Week 1)
**Week 1 Activities:**
- Kickoff meeting with all stakeholders
- Project team introduction
- Communication plan establishment
- Project timeline confirmation
- Success metrics definition
- **Deliverables**: Project charter, communication plan, initial timeline

#### Phase 2: Technical Integration (Weeks 2-4)
**Technical Setup Activities:**
- EHR system integration
- Security configuration
- User account provisioning
- Data migration planning
- Testing environment setup
- **Deliverables**: Technical integration plan, security assessment, test plan

#### Phase 3: User Training and Preparation (Weeks 3-6)
**Training Activities:**
- Administrator training sessions
- Super-user training program
- End-user training materials
- Training schedule coordination
- Certification program setup
- **Deliverables**: Training materials, certification program, training schedule

#### Phase 4: Pilot Deployment (Weeks 5-8)
**Pilot Activities:**
- Pilot user group selection
- Controlled rollout
- Performance monitoring
- Issue identification and resolution
- Feedback collection and analysis
- **Deliverables**: Pilot results, issue log, feedback summary

#### Phase 5: Full Deployment (Weeks 7-10)
**Deployment Activities:**
- Organization-wide rollout
- User activation and adoption
- Support escalation procedures
- Performance monitoring
- Issue resolution
- **Deliverables**: Deployment checklist, user activation metrics, support procedures

#### Phase 6: Optimization and Handoff (Weeks 9-12)
**Optimization Activities:**
- Performance optimization
- Workflow refinement
- Success metrics review
- Customer success handoff
- Ongoing support transition
- **Deliverables**: Optimization report, success metrics, customer success plan

### Mid-Market Onboarding Process (4-6 weeks)

#### Week 1: Technical Setup
- Basic EHR integration

- Initial training session

#### Weeks 2-3: Training and Preparation
- Administrator training

#### Weeks 4-5: Pilot and Optimization
- Pilot deployment

- Issue resolution

#### Week 6: Full Launch
- Organization-wide rollout
- Success metrics validation

### SMB Onboarding Process (2-3 weeks)

- Full deployment
- User training
