## Crisis Communication Plan

### Crisis Communication Team
- **Crisis Communications Lead**: CEO or designated executive
- **Technical Lead**: CTO for technical issues
- **Legal Counsel**: For regulatory and legal matters
- **Customer Success**: For customer impact communication
- **PR Agency**: For external communication coordination

### Crisis Communication Protocols

#### Security or Privacy Incident
1. **Immediate Response (0-4 hours)**
   - Internal crisis team activation
   - Initial fact gathering and assessment
   - Customer notification preparation
   - Regulatory compliance procedures

2. **Short-term Response (4-24 hours)**
   - Customer communication deployment
   - Media response preparation
   - Regulatory notification (if required)
   - Technical remediation initiation

3. **Ongoing Communication (24+ hours)**
   - Regular update communications
   - Media briefings and interviews
   - Customer support and assistance
   - Resolution communication

#### Product or Service Issues
1. **Initial Assessment**
   - Issue impact evaluation
   - Customer communication needs
   - Media attention likelihood
   - Response strategy development

2. **Communication Execution**
   - Customer notification and support
   - Status page updates
   - Media response (if warranted)
   - Internal communication coordination
